My Top Three Movies of All Time

Saving Private Ryan

A Story about courage, and a peek into the historical act that saved the world from a dictator.

Out of Africa

A story of a Danish Entrepreneur, who ventured into Africa in the 1930's. He got more than he bargained for, almost losing his wife in the process

Troy

As legend has it, Troy the inpeneratable city was destroyed by the Spartans after a protracted war lasting 10 years. Archillies lead the attack on the Tojan city after entering the inside through a huge wooden horse. His mission was not only to sack Troy but also rescue Helen who had been abducted by the trojans.
